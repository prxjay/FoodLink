About FoodLink

Connecting communities through food sharing and reducing waste

Our Mission

FoodLink is a community-driven platform that connects people who have excess food with those who need it. We believe that no food should go to waste while people go hungry. Our mission is to create a sustainable food sharing ecosystem that benefits everyone in the community.

Through our platform, individuals, restaurants, and organizations can easily post surplus food, and community members can claim it, creating a win-win situation that reduces food waste and helps those in need.

What We Offer

Food Posting

Easily post surplus food with details like type, quantity, location, and contact information. Help reduce food waste in your community.

Food Claiming

Browse available food donations and claim items you need. Connect with donors and arrange pickup or delivery.

Community Building

Join a network of like-minded individuals working together to create a more sustainable and caring community.

How It Works

1. Post Food: Have extra food? Post it on our platform with details about what you have, where you are, and how to contact you.

2. Browse Available Food: Check out what food is available in your area and find items that interest you.

3. Claim Food: Contact the donor and arrange to pick up or receive the food you've claimed.

4. Build Community: Connect with people and help create a more sustainable community through food sharing.
