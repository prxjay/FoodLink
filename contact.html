Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I post food on FoodLink?
A: Simply log in to your account, click "Post Food", fill in the details about your food donation, and submit. Your post will be visible to the community immediately.

Q: Is there a fee to use FoodLink?
A: No, FoodLink is completely free to use. We believe in making food sharing accessible to everyone in the community.

Q: How do I claim food from other users?
A: Browse the available food listings, click on items you're interested in, and contact the donor directly using the provided contact information.

Q: What if I have a problem with a food donation?
A: Please contact our support team immediately. We take all reports seriously and will work to resolve any issues quickly.
